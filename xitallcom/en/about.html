Crowd positioning

Xitall serves Chinese young men.
They have the knowledge and cultivation,
have independent personality, are good at thinking,
can be accepted by the traditional culture, can quickly accept new things,
have certain requirements for their material and spiritual quality of life,
constantly improve themselves, and have the courage to undertake family and social responsibilities.

Product Positioning

Xitall's products include
clothing, footwear, bags, accessories, etc.
Self-confidence is the soul of Xitall.
Everything that brings you confidence and ritual is yours.

1.Design

Committed to highlighting the temperament of men's self-confidence and self-confidence,
shaping young tough guys in the new era:
they have dreams and pursuits,
have ideas, dare to act, dare to take responsibility, are not afraid of suffering,
humble and gentle to others, demand restraint from themselves,
and constantly enrich themselves.
Now some young people are weak, confused, afraid to take responsibility,
and there is no masculinity in their image temperament.
Xitall wants to change this atmosphere through the expression of products .

2.Visual style

The version is more compact and compact,
and reserves space in the necessary places,
making people feel mentally capable, taking into account the eyes and self-experience of others.
The color is mainly black, white and gray,
and the mysterious and low-key has rich connotation and imagination.
Eliminate the extra pattern decoration,
giving people a sense of security.

3.Pricing strategy

Xitall guarantees quality with good design and craftsmanship,
and every detail can stand up to scrutiny.
But unlike luxury goods, the brand is positioned as a young group.
Xitall's mission is to accompany and motivate them to grow.
Excessive pricing will keep them out.
Xitall's philosophy is to motivate users to be self-disciplined,
too thin or obese people will be excluded,
so you may not be able to buy Xitall products if you have money.
But if you change your position by yourself,
Xitall will give special rights and benefits.
